About Emergence Advisors

Emergence Advisors is a privately held consulting firm based in Laguna Niguel, CA. We specialize in financial and business management consulting services, serving multiple cities in Orange County and surrounding areas.

With decades of combined experience, we bring time-tested solutions that matter and make a difference. Our focus is on streamlining operations, optimizing strategy, and unlocking growth for forward-thinking businesses.

Our Expertise

As management consultants (SIC 8742, NAICS 541611), we provide operating counsel and assistance to managements of private, nonprofit, and public organizations. Our comprehensive approach addresses strategic and organizational planning, financial planning and budgeting, marketing objectives and policies, information systems planning, and human resource management.
